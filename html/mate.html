My favourite comedy:

Monty Python and The Holy Grail

The movie is silly and surreal take on the story King Arthur. The movies humor is easily its best part. Not only is the writing hilarious, with many easily quotable scenes, but some of it comes from the creator's creative solutions to their low budget.

Directors: Terry Gilliam, Terry Jones

Writers: Graham Chapman, John Cleese, Eric Idle, Terry Gilliam, Terry Jones, Michael Palin
